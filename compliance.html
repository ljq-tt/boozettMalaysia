Import Compliance

Buyer Responsibility

The import of alcohol is regulated differently in every country. It is the buyer's sole responsibility to ensure that the importation of alcohol is legal in their country of residence and to comply with all applicable import regulations, duties and restrictions.

Export Controls

Maison Han complies with all applicable export control laws. We provide accurate product descriptions, country of origin and HS codes on all customs documentation.

Restricted Markets

We do not ship to jurisdictions where the sale or import of alcohol is prohibited. If you are unsure whether we can ship to your location, contact orders@boozett.top before placing an order.

Customs Duties

Import duties and taxes are not included in your order total and are the responsibility of the recipient. Rates vary by country, product type and declared value. We recommend consulting your local customs authority before ordering.
