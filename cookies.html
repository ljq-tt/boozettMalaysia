Cookie Policy

What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. We use them to ensure the site works correctly and to understand how it is used.

Cookies We Use

  • Essential cookies — required for the site to function (cart state, age verification flag, language and currency preferences). Cannot be disabled.
  • Analytics cookies — Cloudflare Web Analytics, privacy-friendly and cookieless by default. No personal data collected.
  • Payment cookies — set by Stripe during checkout for fraud prevention. Governed by Stripe's privacy policy.

What We Do Not Use

  • Advertising or tracking cookies
  • Third-party social media cookies
  • Cross-site tracking

Managing Cookies

Essential cookies cannot be disabled as they are necessary for the site to function. You can clear cookies at any time through your browser settings. Note that clearing cookies will reset your language, currency and cart preferences.
